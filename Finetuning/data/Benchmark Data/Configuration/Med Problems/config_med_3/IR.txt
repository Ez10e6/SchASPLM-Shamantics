Batteries: SmallCell (Endurance 30), MediumCell (Endurance 50), LongLife (Endurance 80).
Select one frame, one motor, one battery.
HighTorque motors require LongLife battery.
Lightweight frame is incompatible with HighTorque motor.
LongLife battery requires Reinforced frame.
SmallCell battery only works with Eco motor.
MediumCell battery works with Eco or Standard motors.
